Methodology

How It Works

A structured, transparent engagement process from scoping to verified remediation.

01

Scope

We define target assets, testing windows, compliance requirements, and rules of engagement collaboratively. Every detail is documented before a single packet is sent.

02

Test

Expert testers execute manual and tool-assisted testing, simulating real-world attacker techniques against your systems. We go beyond automated scanning to find what others miss.

03

Report

You receive a detailed report with prioritized findings, evidence, compliance mapping, and precise remediation guidance. Clear, actionable, and auditor-ready from day one.

04

Remediate

We verify your fixes with targeted retesting and provide an updated attestation letter for your auditors. The engagement isn't done until your team is confident in every fix.
