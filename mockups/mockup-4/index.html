How It Works

A structured, transparent engagement process from scoping to verified remediation.

1

Scope

We define target assets, testing windows, compliance requirements, and rules of engagement collaboratively.

2

Test

Expert testers execute manual and tool-assisted testing, simulating real-world attacker techniques against your systems.

3

Report

You receive a detailed report with prioritized findings, evidence, compliance mapping, and precise remediation guidance.

4

Remediate
